You're invited! A party for the best of society, full of notable and influential people, coming together to connect, catch up, and have fun. Despite the organisers' continual insistence however, it's an open secret that this event is very popular with vampires, and that things can get, well, _bloody_.

# Setup

The host prepares a subset of a normal deck of cards. Alternate randomly picking red and black cards until there is 1 card for each participant (don't forget to count yourself!).

_Alternatively, you may want to specifically pick out a set of cards, possibly from more decks to get more copies of each card, possibly more than the number of players for a bit of spice._

Then, each attendee draws a card from that set. This is who you are -- look at it and keep it, but don't show anyone else! A red card means you're a Vampire, and a black one means you're a Human. The card's rank determines your goal here.

|Rank|Role|Description|
|-
| A  | Slayer       | There are attendees who wish to do harm. Stop them. Kill them first if you need to.
| 1  | Fool         | Try to convince everyone that you're a vampire.
| 2  | Lover        | Pick someone (or multiple!) during setup. You think they're really cool/important, and want to support them! Keep them alive and help them with their goals. If they die/get turned, feel free to avenge them in whatever way you feel is appropriate :)
| 3  | Gossip       | Spread rumours and misinformation, including but not limited to who's a vampire/human.
| 4  | Medium       | Help the dead fulfil their goals. Their goals are your goals.
| 5  | Heretic      | You are convinced that no one here is a vampire, maybe except you. You are convinced nothing bad will happen. Rationalise anything contrary as something mundane.
| 6  | Paranoid     | You suspect everyone of being a vampire, maybe except you. Publicly accuse attendees of being one!
| 7  | Noble        | Uphold peace and the social order. Try to stop killings and turnings, and denounce those that try to. Denounce those that are found to be vampires, too.
| 8  | Stargazer    | Try to publicly guess who's a vampire or human, and publicly predict turnings and killings! You don't have to be correct :p
| 9  | Journalist   | You want to find all starting vampires. Take selfies with them! For bonus points, get a photo of all of them together!
| 10 | Jester       | Spark chaos! See if you can convince people to turn and kill each other. Try to get yourself killed! No reason or motivation beyond that it'll be funny (':>
| J  | Bloodthirsty | Whether hunger or revenge or something else, you have an overwhelming desire to kill and/or turn someone. Maybe it's someone specific you pick during setup, maybe it's just whoever is convenient. Let's just hope you can get away with it.
| Q  | Fanatic      | You love vampirism! You think it's the best thing in the world and that everyone would love being one. Try to get as many people turned into vampires as you can. Definitely try to get turned if you're not a vampire already.
| K  | Devil        | Black: you want all vampires gone. Red: you want all humans gone.

_Alternatively, come up with more roles yourself!_

Make sure everyone understands how the game works (the next section!), then give everyone some time to come up with their character:

- What your title(s) are
- What everyone has heard about you
- Which other attendees you have met before, and under what circumstances those were
- Secretly, why your goal is what it is

Once everyone is ready, go around and introduce yourselves! Then, the host announces the start of the party!

# Playing

Once during the game (including while you are a Ghost) you may **kill someone**. Get the attention of everyone in the area, such as by ringing a glass, and announce who you kill and how. The victim becomes a Ghost.

While an alive Vampire, once during the game, you may also **attempt to turn someone**. You two must be alone. Tell your victim that you turn them -- they become a Vampire (if they aren't already).

As a Ghost, everyone can tell you're dead. Once, you may **see someone's card**. Ask someone, and they show you their card.

# Wrapping Up

When the party wraps up, reveal what your card is, and see how well you have fulfilled your goal!
