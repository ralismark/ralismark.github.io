I heard about this book _years_ ago and only finally read it recently.

As a whole, the book was such a comfort read.
The bulk was just episodes of life aboard the ship and I _loved_ that so much.
Such a kaleidoscope of the best parts of people, joy and hope and looking after each other.

It's so unashamedly queer omg 🥰
I love the Aandrisk, I love their family system, I love how intimacy is so comfortable and normal.

The characters are all so well done and so distinct, I love Kizzy especially.

During the Rosemary/Sissix chapter I was cheering them on SO MUCH and it's genuinely made me realise how much fun romance in fiction can be.
I was like "HELL YEAH GAY INTERSPECIES ROMANCE IN THE BOOK I'M READING"

In the best way, I WANT MORE.
There's definitely stuff that i would've wanted developed.
Every single chapter makes me yearn for a follow-up chapter seeing how to characters react in the wake of what happened.
More Rosemary/Sissix!
More of singular Ohan!
More of what's up with Corbin at the end!

I guess this is when I head to AO3 :>
